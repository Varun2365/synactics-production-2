What is Website Hosting?

Web hosting is the essential service that grants websites a virtual presence on the internet. It involves renting server space to store website files, ensuring constant accessibility and reliable performance. This digital infrastructure allows businesses and individuals to showcase their content globally. With various hosting types catering to different needs, web hosting providers contribute to the seamless functioning, security, and scalability of websites, forming the bedrock of an effective online presence.
